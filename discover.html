Hawker Streets Ground Floor

Bustling, Noisy and crowded. This is Malaysia hawker streets, taste unique foods and experience a once in a lifetime experience here!

The Arcade Level 4

Looking for a fun time? Come to the arcade and enjoy over 50+ machines from old to new generation to relive your childhood!

Topmall Arcade is an entertainment center with many arcade machines from the olden times to the most modern machine available to gamers and visitors to enjoy their time here. Come visit the arcade now to be lost in the games!

Chinatown South Entrance

Lively, colorful and beautiful Topmall Chinatown is located at the outskirts of the Topmall Megamall. Here you can find numerous local artist selling unique clothings, accessories and crafts.

The Topamll Chinatown attraction is made up of cultural and traditional buldings inspired by traditional chinese market, in here you can find sprawling flea markets, incredible artistic goods and amazing foods from the chinese culture!

Visit the replica Ching Chong Temple built hand in hand by the famous architect Sum Ting Wong and artist all over the world.

About Us

One of the greatest mall in Malaysia

The Topmall sdn bhd is a Malaysia based organization that deals in property development that dates back to the year 2021 when our founder Wang Te Fak who had a vision to create the perfect megamall that puts the customer first to provide a higher quality of life to the locals while also stimulating the local economy.

Us Topmall sdn bhd have one and only one mission that is to provide an out of the world megamall experience to our visitors in every base. By gathering talents all over the world we wish to realize this mission and revolutionize the concept of megamall.

Now Topmall megamall is located in the heart of Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur standing over 400,000 sq ft and over 200 retail stores and food and beverages outlet. Working in hand with famous architect Sum Ting Wong, our mall layout is meticulously planned to guarantee our visitors comfort and safety while also maintaining its modern architecture design with cutting edge technology and high-quality supplementary services.

With the great success from our megamall, we aim to further achieve greater goals to realize our founder vision. Currently, we are focusing our resources to raise another megamall to further expand to every corner of Malaysia and gathering like minded to grow as the largest organization in Malaysia.

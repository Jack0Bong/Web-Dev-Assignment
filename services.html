Our services and support

Have a question? Need assistance? Do approach our support reception desk at the ground floor for assistances! Our staff are trained profession who can solve any inquiries and assistance as needed.


The reception desk can be found on the ground floor just besides the south entrance, if you are still confused on how to reach there feel free to approach any one of our security staff for directions, they are ready to warmly assist you!


Unable to reach us physically? Then contact us through this number +60 3123852919 for inquires and assistance.

MALL SERVICES

ATM

ATMs can be found on each floor outside of Washroom with 4 different banks available such as YamBank, PrivateBank, Leong Hong Bank, Christian Bank

Located At

Every Floor, Outside Washroom

WHEELCHAIR

Wheelchair services can be found at the support reception desk, RM40 deposit is required for non Malaysians.

Located At

Level Ground, South Entrance, Service Center

SHOPPING BAG

Shopping Bag / Groceries Bag can be purchased at the support reception desk for RM 4.50 or at the Supermarket at Level 3.

Located At

Level Ground, Level 3 , Service Center, Topmall Supermarket

GIFT WRAPPING

Complimentary Gift Wrapping service is also available at the support reception desk for any item purchased in the mall.

Located At

Level Ground, South Entrance, Service Center

Mobility Scooter

Mobility Scooter can also be rented at the support reception desk and at the South entrance.

Located At

South Entrance, Level Ground, Service Center

Lost and Found

Found or Lost an item? Come to the support reception desk to claim or drop off any valuable items.

Located At

Level Ground, South Entrance, Service Center

Sanitizer

Sanitizer is available all across the mall, feel free to use it as you fit. Refill is done every 5 hours

Located At

Washroom, Entrance
